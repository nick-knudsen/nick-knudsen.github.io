I built this site to serve as a portfolio for my various interests, principally cocktails, coding, and photography. I graduated from the University of Vermont in 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Data Science and a minor in math. In my spare time I spend a lot of time outdoors, hiking, birding, camping, biking, and snowboarding.

I currently am pursuing a passion for cocktail creation, and work as the head bartender at Pizzeria Verita, a Neapolitan restaurant in Burlington, Vermont. I am interested in both technique and ingredient driven cocktails, with a focus on using local ingredients, reducing waste, and general sustainability in the bar program as a whole.

When it comes to computer science, I am especially interested in large and novel datasets, complex systems, evolutionary computation, and neural networks. I am constantly working on several coding projects, both in order to hone my skills in my areas of expertise, as well as to develop new ones and broaden the range of my knowledge and experience.

My most used tool is almost certainly a camera. I shoot both digital and film, and carry a camera with me most places. My favorite subjects include environmental portraiture, birds, macro, long exposure landscape, and astrophotography.

Please contact me for a copy of my resume, or with any comments, requests, or questions you may have!
